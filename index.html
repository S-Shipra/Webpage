About Us

The Training and Placement Office (TPO) at Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute (VJTI) is dedicated to facilitating the placement process for students. We collaborate with various companies to organize campus recruitment drives and provide career guidance and training to students.

Our team consists of experienced professionals who work tirelessly to ensure that students are well-prepared for the challenges of the job market. We strive to bridge the gap between academia and industry, helping students transition seamlessly into their professional lives.
